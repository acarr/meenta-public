Submission and Delivery Deadlines

How does meenta manage your sample? Below is a diagram of the timeline of events that will impact your sample.

Submit Your Order

Date you purchased your sample lanes.

Cancellation Period

Within 48 hours of the Order Date, either the User or the Host can cancel for any reason.

Sample Arrives at Host

The sample must arrive at the host two days before the run date.

Host Runs Sample

This is the date when your sample will start and is run on the instrument.

Data Delivery

Eight days after the run date your data is available to download.

Cancelation Policy

In the first 48 hours after a purchase a sample order can be cancelled, with a full refund. After the 48 hour period, a sample will be charged.

Pipeline Dates

Here are some dates to think about before you purchase.

  • Order Date Date you purchased.
  • Delivery Date This is the date that the sample must arrive at the host.
  • Run Date This is the date when you sample will start its run on the instrument.
  • Data Delivery Date This is the date you sample data will be available for download.

Penalties

Yes, Meenta has penalties.

Late Samples If a sample arrives late, after the ‘Delivery Date’, the user will be charged a 10% fee. Why a fee? The host will need to reschedule your sample in their LIM system.

Data Delivery If the host does not deliver sample data by the ‘Data Delivery’ date, then Meenta assesses the host a 10% fee.
