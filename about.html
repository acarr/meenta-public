About Meenta

Accelerating Science Research Outcomes Enabling Personalized Medicine

Why Meenta

There is no viable alternative to trial-and-error medicine for many diseases today. We still now little about their causes, about the biological markers of their presence or stage, and about the factors that influence the effectiveness of possible remedies.

Meenta is here to enable life science acceleration - enabling scientists to speed up their research and discoveries to empower the next phase of Personalized Medicine .
